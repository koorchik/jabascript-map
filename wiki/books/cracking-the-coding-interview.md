# Cracking the Coding Interview

Gayle Laakmann McDowell's interview-prep bible. Viktor used it for his own Google interview prep and did *all* the problems — but his contrarian take is that the underrated gold is the two introductions: what interviews actually expect from you, and the algorithmic-complexity primer. The tasks themselves you can just as well grind on LeetCode or AlgoExpert.
