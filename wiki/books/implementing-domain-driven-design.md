# Implementing Domain-Driven Design

Vaughn Vernon's hands-on companion to Evans's DDD — the book that shows exactly how to structure the code. Viktor recommends it alongside Evans, but insists you start with Evans: people who read only Vernon tend to mistake code structure for DDD, when the conceptual idea is the whole point. The more practically-focused of the two DDD books on his shelf.
