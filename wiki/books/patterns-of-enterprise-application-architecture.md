# Patterns of Enterprise Application Architecture

Martin Fowler's catalog of strategic architecture patterns — Active Record, Data Mapper, Unit of Work and friends. Viktor's universal pattern recommendation: don't be scared by the word "Enterprise". Unlike tactical GoF patterns, these are strategic patterns you'll almost never implement yourself but will meet inside every ORM and framework — the book teaches you how your tools are built. In his design trio it complements [[clean-architecture]] (it shows how to actually realize the layers) and [[domain-driven-design]] (value objects and the rest).
