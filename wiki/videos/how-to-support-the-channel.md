# How to Support the Channel (Sponsorship Tiers)

> Original: "Як підтримати канал (варіанти спонсорства)?" — https://youtu.be/po0gj69V32E

A short housekeeping video walking through the channel's four YouTube sponsorship tiers. The author's "first rule" of choosing a tier: pick the one where the monthly payment notification doesn't annoy you but genuinely makes you happy to be helping the channel grow. He stresses that becoming a sponsor should be followed by joining the private community chat. See [[channel-and-author]].

## Key takeaways
- **"Кодер Малюк" (Coder Baby), 35 UAH/month** — already meaningful support; grants access to the closed sponsors' Telegram chat, influence over upcoming videos (he shares plans, runs internal polls, takes feedback), and top-priority replies to comments — fast, or if not fast then detailed.
- **"Архітектор Байтів" (Architect of Bytes), 200 UAH/month** — adds exclusive videos: Q&A follow-ups on existing videos, planned webinars and workshops; questions from sponsors at this tier can be turned into dedicated videos with a detailed walkthrough.
- **"Суперсила каналу" (Channel Superpower)** — the most popular tier by subscriber count; adds off-topic bonus content (side-topic, unedited videos — one car video already exists, which he opened to all sponsor tiers as a sample) plus planned live-coding sessions showing how he writes code and his thinking along the way.
- **Maximum support tier** — only two extra perks, deliberately: (1) request a topic and he records a video on it as first priority (it may stay sponsor-only if the topic is very specialized, but it gets made first), and (2) exclusive gifts from him once a year, which he planned to send to everyone that year.
- Final reminder: after subscribing at any tier, don't forget to join the private chat.
