# Vertex AI

Google Cloud's machine-learning platform. Viktor speaks about it first-hand — he worked alongside the Vertex AI team during his years at Google — and calls it the most end-to-end ML platform in any cloud: training, fine-tuning, deployment and serving models through an API in one place ([[qa-2-answering-questions]]). His insider note on quality assurance for AI features there: regression frameworks plus lots of dogfooding, since ML behavior can't be pinned down by conventional test suites alone — consistent with his broader point that ML engineering is permanent research-mode uncertainty ([[ai-and-jobs]]).
