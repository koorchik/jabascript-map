# The Channel and Its Author

**jabascript** is a Ukrainian-language YouTube channel about programming, databases, networking, security and engineering craft, run by **Viktor** — an engineer whose path runs from an economics degree to Senior SWE at Google. The channel's founding idea, laid out in [[qa-and-plans-for-2024]], is an **evergreen knowledge base for Ukrainian-speaking engineers**: fundamentals-first videos meant to stay useful for years, deliberately watchable oldest-first without going stale. It's his *second* attempt at YouTube — a retro-gaming test channel earned only ~300 subscribers in a year of weekly streams (but served as his OBS/gear lab), while jabascript hit ~19k subscribers and 167 sponsors in its first year.

## Who Viktor is

His biography, told first-hand across [[qa-and-plans-for-2024]] and [[qa-2-answering-questions]]:

- **Economist by degree** (Shevchenko University), in IT since 2003.
- **Started in information security** — an infosec intern at BMS Consulting — and admin of a 250-computer dorm network, automated with Perl.
- **Mail.ua, 4 years** — FUSE filesystems, PXE deployment; independently re-invented 8 GoF patterns there before ever reading the book ([[five-star-books]]).
- **Portaone** — VoIP systems.
- **Co-founded WebbyLab in 2011** with Anton Morozov. During the full-scale war, WebbyLab donated 100% of its first-year war profit and then ran a volunteer fund ([[qa-2-answering-questions]]).
- **Google, Senior SWE on [[google-cloud-run|Cloud Run]] since 2021.** Inside Google he absorbed gRPC-like internal RPC/protobuf, Bazel's internal ancestor, design-doc culture (the Google Cloud console is a ~12M-LOC Angular app with micro-frontends), API design and frontend test harnesses — and watched 3 reorgs in 4 years ([[voice-5-why-i-left-google]]).
- **Quit Google in ~2025** after ~4 years. He got what he came for — confirmed staff-level by Google standards, the CV line that eases future startup fundraising — but catastrophically lacked time for his cybersecurity PhD, volunteering, sick parents (father's heart, mother's cancer surgery) and his own health; the announcement was recorded in his car outside a hospital. The 4-year stock cliff had also just passed, and a $13k signing bonus was forfeit because the invasion blocked his contractual Poland relocation ([[voice-5-why-i-left-google]]).

A telling Google episode: his **Application Canvas** project was praised by top management — then shut down, resetting his L6 promotion track ([[voice-5-why-i-left-google]]).

## PhD, pet projects, plans

After a long ТЦК/reservation saga, he **enrolled in a cybersecurity PhD (аспірантура)** — giving up his Google-provided reservation for flexibility — returning to the field where his career began; he plans to teach infosec courses ([[qa-2-answering-questions]]). Post-Google plans: health, more YouTube, the PhD, volunteering, and a flexible schedule in Kyiv ([[voice-5-why-i-left-google]]). Though his Google contract obliged relocation to Warsaw (where his family already was), he sees his kids' future in Ukraine ([[qa-2-answering-questions]]).

Pet projects: **mytalks.net** — a "Behance for speakers" — and a planned autofocus tool for his screen-less Blackmagic camera via its API ([[qa-2-answering-questions]]), the same camera he later wrote a controller app for in [[vibe-coded-mobile-app]].

2024 plans from [[qa-and-plans-for-2024]]: an HTTPS deep-dive, a Cloud Run series, 20–25k subscribers, and integrations **only for products he already recommends** (first: Mate Academy) — plus a hard commitment to authentic, self-edited content: in the Sora era, authenticity only gets more valuable. Production trivia: the first year was shot entirely on an iPhone 13 Pro Max with the NDI Camera app, later a Fujifilm X-E4; he streams from an Asus Zenbook, not a MacBook.

## Sponsorship model

[[how-to-support-the-channel]] documents the four tiers, from 35 UAH/mo "Кодер Малюк" (closed Telegram chat, influence on video plans, priority replies) through "Архітектор Байтів" (exclusive Q&A videos, webinars) and the most popular "Суперсила каналу" (unedited off-topic videos like [[awd-systems-compared]]) up to the max tier (first-priority topic requests, yearly gifts). His guiding rule: **pick the tier whose payment notification makes you happy** — and always join the private chat. The sponsor tiers directly shape the channel's meta formats: voice-message episodes and off-topic videos exist for sponsors (see [[channel-meta]]).
