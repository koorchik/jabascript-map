# AI & Vibe-coding

The channel's AI arc runs from skepticism to mastery without ever dropping its standards. It starts in 2023 with a Google I/O watch party where Bard flails at a Node.js task GPT-4 nails, continues with comedy shorts in which Viktor Socratically breaks Gemini's common sense (the car wash that requires the car), and matures — through a personal binary-search-in-file benchmark applied to every model — into full vibe-coding livestreams: `CLAUDE.md`-encoded standards, plan mode, parallel [[claude-code|Claude Code]] instances, and a Blackmagic camera app shipped in two evenings. The constant across the arc: AI is a junior that scales like juniors, so the engineer's real leverage is architecture, standards and the [[deep-learning-of-fundamentals|deep understanding]] needed to correct the machine.
